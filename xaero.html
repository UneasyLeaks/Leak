Rebuild After Download

Download all five parts into one folder, rebuild the archive for your operating system, then extract it.

Windows Command Prompt

copy /b xaero-map-files.tar.gz.part-aa+xaero-map-files.tar.gz.part-ab+xaero-map-files.tar.gz.part-ac+xaero-map-files.tar.gz.part-ad+xaero-map-files.tar.gz.part-ae xaero-map-files.tar.gz
tar -xzf xaero-map-files.tar.gz

macOS

cat xaero-map-files.tar.gz.part-* > xaero-map-files.tar.gz
tar -xzf xaero-map-files.tar.gz

Linux

cat xaero-map-files.tar.gz.part-* > xaero-map-files.tar.gz
tar -xzf xaero-map-files.tar.gz

Move the extracted xaeros files contents into the matching Xaero world-map folder in your Minecraft game directory.

  • Windows: %APPDATA%\.minecraft\XaeroWorldMap
  • macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/XaeroWorldMap
  • Linux: ~/.minecraft/XaeroWorldMap
